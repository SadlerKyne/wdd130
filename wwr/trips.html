Available Trips

RioRio

A gentle float down the rio rio, This is a great trip for anyone wanting to get experience in rafting and learn the ropes.

  • Duration: 3 - 4 Hours
  • Difficulty: Beginner
  • Price: $300
  • Group Size: 6

RattleSnakes Back

This 2 day adventure down the rattlesnakes back is not for the faint of heart but is a trip that you will not forget. We reccomend this trip to those adventurers who have been on a few trips and know the ins and outs.

  • Duration: 2 days
  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Price: $800
  • Group Size: 6 - 18

Mickies Hottub

Mickies Hottub is a build your own adventure this can be as long or as short as you want. You will have to show your strength to the guide before being allowed to take on this river. Anyone wanting to do this trip must have completed an Advanced trip with the guides prior.

  • Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Price: $1400
  • Group Size: 6 - 18
